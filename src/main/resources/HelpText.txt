This WordPress Podcaster is designed to have a easier way publishing new Podcast to your Website.


How it works:


If your starting the program the first time you have to link your WordPress-Website and login to it.

If you have already done this:
- Create a new Podcast
- Choose your File, which you're going to upload
- Give it a title
- Click Save and Publish it to your Website

Editing:
You can edit a Podcast by choosing your Podcast in the Table
- Choose your Podcast
- Click Edit
- After your changes click Save

Removing:
If you don't want to have a Podcast on your Website anymore just delete it
- Choose the Podcast
- Click Remove
- Verify it by clicking on Ok
