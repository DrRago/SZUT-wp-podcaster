This WordPress Podcaster is designed to have a easier way publishing new Podcast to your Website.


How it works:

If you want to create a new post:
- Click on 'Add new Posts'
- There you can set default values for all your upcoming posts
- To add a post you have to click on 'Add post to queue'
- Then you can give your post a title
- Choose your File, which you're going to upload
- Click 'Add to queue' and then 'Upload posts' to submit your posts to your WordPress server

Removing:
If you don't want to have a Podcast on your Website anymore just delete it
- Choose the Podcast
- Click Remove
- Verify it by clicking on Ok

If you want to change your Upload server, simply click 'Change Upload Server'.
To change your WordPress server, click on 'Logout from WordPress'.
